## Idea
CiviqAI is an AI Municipal Intelligence Platform that transforms reactive complaint management into proactive city governance. It auto-prioritizes complaints by risk and citizen impact, detects duplicates, predicts infrastructure failures before they occur, analyzes damage via computer vision, optimizes workforce deployment, and flags fraudulent completion claims — all visible on a live city Digital Twin. Governments resolve faster, spend smarter, and act before citizens suffer.
